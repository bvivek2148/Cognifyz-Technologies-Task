Trending Cricket News

In a historic achievement at the ICC Cricket World Cup 2023, Virat Kohli became the first batter to score 50 ODI centuries, breaking Sachin Tendulkar's record. His magnificent innings of 117 runs against New Zealand in the semi-final helped India secure their place in the World Cup final. The milestone came in his 279th ODI innings, making him one of the most consistent performers in cricket history. Throughout the tournament, Kohli has been in exceptional form, scoring over 700 runs and breaking numerous records along the way. His remarkable achievement was celebrated by cricket fans worldwide, with Sachin Tendulkar himself present at the stadium to witness this historic moment.
